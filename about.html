About The Founder

Sambuddha Biswas

Sambuddha Biswas is a high school senior at D.P.S R.K. Puram. He has been playing music since he was 5 years old and is also passionate about programming and web development. Knowing how hard it is to locate fellow musicians, Sambuddha wanted to create a platform where musicians could come explore and make connections.
